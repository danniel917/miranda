Massage Therapy

Massage therapy consists primarily of hands-on manipulation of the soft tissues of the body, specifically, the muscles, connective tissue, tendons, ligaments and joints for the purpose of optimizing health. Massage therapy treatment has a therapeutic effect on the body and optimizes health and well-being. Physical function can be developed, maintained and improved.

Conditions treated by Massage Therapy
  • - Muscle Tension and Spams
  • - Back, leg, and neck pain
  • - Carpal tunnel syndrome (repetitive strain)
  • - Fibromyalgia
  • - Edema
  • - Headaches
  • - Pregnancy
  • - Post-surgical rehabilitation
  • - Strains and sprains
  • - Sports injuries
  • - Stress and stress related conditions

Body Sculpt and Reduction

It is a treatment using different machines and hands on techniques that help reduce localized fatty tissue.

Our body stores fat in different ways according to the individual characteristics of each person, gender being one of the most important factors.
